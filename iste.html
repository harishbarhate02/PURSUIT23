Grand Project Expo

Event Description:

 This is a competitive event, called Grand Project Expo, designed for the new and unique ideas implemented as Hardware or Software based projects. There will be only one round. All respective teams have to demonstrate their projects in front of judges on their respective panels with proper explanation & answer the questions asked by them.

Rules :

  1. All participants should have valid ID card.
  2. Physical damage of facility provided to participants will lead to disqualification.
  3. No person can be a part of multiple teams .
  4. Knowledge of participants about theory and presentation would be seen by judging..
  5. Innovativeness and Originality would be seen by judges
