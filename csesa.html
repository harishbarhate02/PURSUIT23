WEB-A-THON

Event Description:

It's Web developmet based event

No.of Rounds - 2

Pre-requisite:

Laptop (if available)

Round 1:

1) In first 1 hour participants have to choose from the given problem statement and build the rough idea of how they will implement it in next 3 hours.

2) It will be the judging round. It will be the eliminating round.

Round 2:

Participants will get another 2 hours for implementing their idea. It will be the eliminating round.

Round 3:

1) This round will be the final round.

2) Succeeded participants will get 1 hour 30 minutes to build their website as per given suggestions or can modify according to them.

Rules :

  1. i) Participation should be in group of two.
  2. ii) Only selected participants from Round-1 will be qualified to Round-2 and same for Round-3.
  3. iii) They will not be allowed to change their teams.
  4. iv) Judge's decision will be the final decision.
