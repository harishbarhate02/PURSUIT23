About The Event

"Pursuit" is the foremost and most energetic technical festival of SSGMCE, to be held in April of 2023. It is a technical festival with a wide range of events and workshops. Pursuit is a platform for the passionate about technology..

A national technical event is a large-scale gathering of technical experts, researchers, students, and professionals in a particular field to showcase their research, exchange knowledge, and discuss the latest trends and innovations in their respective fields. These events usually include presentations, lectures, seminars, workshops and exhibitions.
